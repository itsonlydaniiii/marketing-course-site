About The Global Talent Co.

We connect you with exceptional marketing talent from around the globe, ensuring you access top-tier professionals who excel in their field. Our recruitment process focuses on finding candidates who not only meet but exceed your expectations.

By partnering with us, you benefit from salaries that are above the local market average, which means your team members are fairly compensated and motivated. This approach helps attract and retain top talent who are dedicated and passionate about their work.

The Problem

“Growth-at-all-costs” was the mantra of 2021 and 2022. Today, it's all about efficient growth: Companies are expected to grow but have less resources to actually make that happen. A massive amount of their marketing budget goes toward staff cost and that staff has become more and more expensive over the years.

Our Solution

Our platform helps you find, vet, hire, pay, and retain amazing marketing talent that costs 50-70% less than you pay for talent in the US or Europe. Our talent is simply amazing: the top 5% of global marketers, all vetted with our AI-powered assessments and manually interviewed by our team.

By partnering with us, you benefit from competitive salaries that are above the local market average, ensuring you receive fair compensation for your skills and experience. Plus, with our focus on efficient growth, you're positioned to join teams that are committed to nurturing your career while effectively managing their resources.
